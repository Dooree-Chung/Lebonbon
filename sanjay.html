Introduction to the game

Introduction

Get ready for a thrilling new gaming experience with Robot Tag! Step into a futuristic world where 2 robots battle it out in an epic game of tag. Use your tagging device to tag the opposite player, while avoiding being tagged yourself. With special abilities, obstacles, and strategic gameplay, Robot Tag offers endless hours of fun and excitement. Join the battle today and see if you have what it takes to become the ultimate tagger!
