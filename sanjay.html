Introduction to the game

Introduction

In Ultimate Tag 2 players battle against each other through numerous levels to try and not be IT at the end of the game. Players use obstacles and power ups to their advantage to try and avoid being tagged at the end of the game. This game showcases vibrant colours, a unique gravity feature and numerous levels to allow players to play accordingly and provide a new environment and interest to the player. “Ultimate Tag” offers a new variant of the popular “Tag” game genre and put’s its own new twist to it. Find out to see if you will become the Ultimate Tagger by playing the game.
